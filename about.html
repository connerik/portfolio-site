About me

My name is Ike Conner, and I am a senior at
Michigan State University pursuing a degree in
Information Science with a focus on Human-
Centered Technologies. I am an aspiring User
Experience Designer and Web Developer with a
strong interest in creating intuitive, meaningful
digital experiences. Throughout my coursework
and project work, I have developed proficiency in
Figma, designing high-fidelity prototypes for
both mobile applications and websites. I am
passionate about the intersection of technology,
design, and human creativity. I believe great
design is fundamentally human, and I strive to
bring that perspective into every project I take on.
